Dunkleosteus Fact


Sometimes described as the Tyrannosaurus of the seas, Dunkleosteus was one of the largest armored fish.(fish with a bony protective covering).
It was as big as an elephant and a viscious hunter in the shallow seas in which it lived. It had the most powerful bite of any fish (except perhaps the megatooth shark).
